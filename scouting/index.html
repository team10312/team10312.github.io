How the team uses this

1. Pit scout in the pits

Use Pit Scouting to record other teams' drivetrain, intake, auto paths, strengths, and repair risks.

2. Match scout from the stands

Use Match Scouting to follow one robot each match and log scoring, defense, fouls, breakdowns, and notes.

3. Review before strategy talks

Use Team Summary and exports to compare teams, spot trends, and prep for alliance selection or match planning.

Data rules

What exports include

  • Match exports include raw per-match counts and notes.
  • Pit exports include the latest submitted pit entry rows.
  • Summary exports include aggregate fuel, tower, defense, and breakdown metrics.

Queued outbox items are stored locally until they sync. Retry the outbox before exporting if you need the newest rows.
